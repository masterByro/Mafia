# Uprising GAME ROLE GUIDE

---

🟢 TOWNFOLK
Eliminate the Uprising and Serial Killer

👨‍⚕️ Healer
**Action:** Heal one player each night
**Info:** Cannot target the same player twice. Can target themselves. Cannot target a Chancellor that has revealed themselves. Cannot heal a death caused by the Jailor or Jester

🕵️ Inquisitor
**Action:** Investigate one player each night
**Info:** Receives clues indicating if the target had “blood” on them. This will detect Insurgent, Serial Killers, Doctors, anyone who has been framed or attacked that night

🚪 Escort
**Action:** Roleblock one player each night
**Info:** Prevents target from using their night ability. Escorting the Serial Killer will result in the Escort's death

👑 Chancellor
**Action:** Reveal their role publicly, in order to have more voting power
**Info:** Chancellor can publicly reveal their role during the day anytime. Once revealed their vote and judgement are worth 3 votes. Once revealed, Healer can no longer heal them

🛡️ Knight
**Action:** Go on alert, killing any night visitor
**Info:** Knight can go on alert 3 times per game, attacking all visitors received on those nights.

💀 Jailor
**Action:** Selects a target to jail during the day, interrogates them and can execute them at night
**Info:** Must select target during daytime. Interrogate the target at night, blocking the target from using their action. Can choose to execute them. Nothing can protect/save the target.

👻 Medium
**Action:** Communicate with the dead at night
**Info:** Dead chat becomes accessible during the night

Watchman
🔭 Watchman
**Action:** Notes down everyone who visits your target's house
**Info:** Select a target's house to watch over. You will keep track of every player who visits this house. This information will become available to you in the morning if you are still alive

## 🏘️ Peasant
**Action:** None
**Info:** No special powers

---

🔴 Uprising: 
Eliminate all Townsfolk and the Serial Killer
All Uprising members can access a private Uprising chatroom at night to co-ordinate their strategy (if a Uprising member is hauled to Jail, they won't be able to speak to the other Uprising members that night)
If a Insurgent dies, the Propagandist will take their place. If there's no Propagandist, the Warden will take their place 

## 🔫 Insurgent
**Action:** Kill one player each night
**Info:** Primary killing role of Uprising

## 🎭 Propagandist
**Action:** Frame a target each night
**Info:** Makes target appear suspicious to investigative roles. Target remains framed until they are investigated (So your night 1 target will show up as suspicious if investigated even on night 3)

## 🧹 Warden
**Action:** Clean a kill
**Info:** If your target dies that night, their will and role will be visible only to you, instead of being publically broadcasted


---

# NEUTRAL CHAOS

⚖️ Executioner
Wins if their target is lynched
**Action:**  None
**Info:** A random town member is selected as your target. If they die of other means, you will turn into the Jester, and your win condition changes

🤡 Jester
Wins if they get lynched
**Ability:** None
**Info:** Can take revenge after death. The night after the lynching, you can murder any player who did not vote 'Innocent' for you

---

# NEUTRAL EVIL

🔪 Serial Killer
Wins when everyone in the town is dead (except the Wanderer)
**Action:** Kill one player each night
**Info:** If targeted by the Escort, you murder the escort instead of your selected target

---

# NEUTRAL BENIGN

🏕️ Wanderer
Wins if alive at end of game
**Action:** Protect yourself at night, for up to 3 nights
**Info:** Your action does not protect you from the Jester or the Jailor


Technical Info:
Action priorities:
1. Jester            Kills target
2. Jailor            Blocks target and can choose to kill target
3. Knight            Kills all following visitors 
4. Escort            Blocks target
5. Healer            At end of round, will heal Attacked target
6. Wanderer          Protected against Attacks
7. Insurgent         Attacks target
8. Serial Killer     Attacks target
9. Propagandist      Frames target
10. Inquisitor       Checks target
11. Warden           Cleans target
12. Watchman         Keeps track of who visits target        

You can use the command !m <message> to write a message that is leave next to every player you murder at night
